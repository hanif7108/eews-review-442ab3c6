Research Narrative

1. Problem Framing

2. Core Contribution — The IDA-PTW Framework

  1. Stage 0 — Ultra-Rapid P-wave Discriminator (URPD): Gradient Boosting pada 7 fitur spektral dari 0.5-s window, AUC 0.988; reduksi blind zone dari 38 km ke 11 km (human) / 4 km (infrastructure).
  2. Stage 1 — XGBoost Intensity Gate: 93.01% accuracy, Damaging Recall 91.09%; routing ke PTW 3–8 s berdasarkan Feature Dichotomy paradigm.
  3. Stage 1.5 — XGBoost Epicentral Distance Regressor: 99.87% routing fidelity; autonomous operation tanpa catalog dependency.
  4. Stage 2 — Ensemble of 412 XGBoost Spectral Regressors (4 PTW × 103 periods) dijangkarkan pada fitur non-saturating (CVAD, CAV, Arias Intensity).

How to read the chart

The three curves

🟢 Green — Full-Wave S-Phase Upper Bound (Theoretical Limit)

⚪ Grey Dashed — Stage-1 Unfiltered (28.2k) Baseline 3s

🔴 Red Solid — Stage-2 Intensity Gated (Top 25k) 3s

The pink shaded band — Accuracy Gain from Intensity Gating

The valley at T ≈ 1 s — Why all three curves dip

RegimeDominated byWhy predictable
T < 0.1 sSite / near-surfaceSpectral centroid, VS30, H/V ratios directly relevant
T ≈ 1 sAll three (hardest)Source + path + site all contribute; non-linear interactions; fundamental period of critical buildings
T > 3 sSource / pathMetadata (distance, depth) correlates strongly
